Amharic Online Keyboard

Instructions:

Typing Method: Type phonetic Latin equivalents (e.g., 's', 'a') and they will convert to Amharic characters ('ሳ').

Examples:

  • me = መ
  • mu = ሙ
  • mi = ሚ
  • ma = ማ
  • mee = ሜ
  • m = ም
  • mo = ሞ
  • mua = ሟ

Press space or punctuation to finalize a character if needed.

You can also click the virtual keys above.
